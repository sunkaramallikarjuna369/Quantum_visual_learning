Pauli-X Gate (NOT Gate)

Easy

What does the Pauli-X (X) gate do? Apply it to both |0⟩ and |1⟩ states.

X|0⟩ = ? | X|1⟩ = ?

Your Answer:

Think of the X gate as a bit flip - it swaps 0 and 1!

Solution:

X|0⟩ = |1⟩ (flips 0 to 1)

X|1⟩ = |0⟩ (flips 1 to 0)

The X gate is the quantum equivalent of the classical NOT gate.
